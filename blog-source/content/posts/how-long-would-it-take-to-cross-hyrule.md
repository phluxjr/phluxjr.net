# calculating how long it would take to cross hyrule for no reason

using link's pariglider, *hyrule is 168 miles long and 198 miles wide*! this is already pretty known, though.

but, for a **proper** *cross-hyrule trek* we need to factor in:

* average human walking speed is **3.1mph**
* ***terrain multipliers.*** *flat hyrule field is easy* but **death mountain** and the **gerudo highlands** would slow you down *significantly*
* sleep (8 hours a night, but where WOULD you sleep?? there's not a town every 20 mins exactly..)
* breaks (probably 2hrs a day for meals etc)
* so you've got roughly 14 usable walking hours a day at 3.1mph = 43.4 miles a day..

### north to south (168 miles) = about 3.9 days 
### west to east (198 miles) = about 4.6 days

and that's *WITHOUT* terrain multipliers.
lets add those now.
death mountain **alone** would probably add a *full day* realistically, you're **not** doing 3.1mph up a volcano. you're going *1mph and* ***crying***. 
and the gerudo highlands are basically a mountain range so west to east is probably closer to 6-8 days if you're routing through there. 
for north to south you factor in lost woods and hyrule castle (good luck with that)
routing through the giant evil castle full of monsters, that'll add what..a day? two days? assuming you survive? which honestly, you won't?
at least gerudo highlands has sunshrooms and zapshrroms..
*the cold though*, that'd be **horrible**..

realistically a full hyrule traverse with terrain penalties, castle detour, and survival concerns is probably 8-10 days total. 
that's a genuine week and a half expedition. 
link is out here doing serious ultramarathon distances and everyone just treats it like a tuesday.
though, this also assumes the average human is an AMAZING climber like link is which..no..that is **NOT** happening unless you have become one with the walls.
so adding the needed detours because, lets be real, you are NOT climbing for like 30 miles upwards total over that 8-10 days, 
that would take it up to at MINIMUM 13 days.

so in conclusion: link is genuinely a freak athlete and we just never appreciate it because the game makes it look easy, it would take 13+ days for any of us to cross it by walking
